IoT Security Framework Quiz

Authored by Mohann Scarlett & Omar Hassan

What are Low processing power IoT devices?

Low processing power IoT devices are devices that rely on Wi-Fi connection and/or apps for controls.

Why are Low processing power IoT devices considered dangerous?

Low processing power IoT devices are dangerous because they are not protected at all and the data collected by these devices are likely not protected by decent encryption due to lack of testing.

What are Medium processing power IoT devices?

Medium processing power IoT devices are devices that do receive updates and may have password authentication.

What are High processing power IoT devices?

High processing power IoT devices are devices that have a complex user interface on the device with other capabilities.
